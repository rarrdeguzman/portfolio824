About

I’m Romeo. I worked for almost five years in advertising, starting as an email developer using platforms like ExactTarget and Eloqua. Later, I explored animation, which was fun and creative, but I returned to email development to continue growing my skills, this time on Adobe Campaign Classic.

In 2024, I stepped out of corporate life to study in Canada at Algonquin College. I completed one year of Interactive Media Management and another year in Cloud Development and Operations.

This website is a place where I share some of my school assignments, hobbies, and personal experiments.
