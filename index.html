About

I'm Romeo and I have almost five years of work experience in an advertising company. My first job was as an email developer, where I was introduced to different campaign platforms such as ExactTarget and Eloqua. I gained a lot of confidence from this experience and then tried a slightly different job opportunity in animation, which was a lot of fun because it felt like playing and required using my imagination to create creative ads. After two years, I decided to pursue email development again because I felt like there was unfinished business from my previous ten months of experience in that role. The responsibilities are still the same—building and deploying emails—but the platform has changed to Adobe Campaign Classic.
